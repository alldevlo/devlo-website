behavior, read:

- `docs/seo-geo-guide.md`

`docs/seo-geo-guide.md`: visible pages are written for B2B buyers first,
French public copy keeps accents, page placement must match the user journey,
and LLM/GEO mechanics belong in metadata, schema, `llms.txt`, or internal docs,
not in buyer-facing copy.

Use a branch and PR. Do not push directly to `main`.
